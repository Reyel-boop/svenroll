Your Guide to Enrollment at SVNHS

Seamless Enrollment: Your Future Starts Here!

Welcome to San Vicente National High School, located in Panabo City, Davao Region! We're thrilled you're considering joining our community. This guide will walk you through the simple steps to enroll in our Senior High School program. Get ready to discover your path!

Enrollment Process Steps:

1

Pre-Registration (Online)

Start your journey by filling out our online pre-registration form. This helps us gather initial information and determine your preferred strand. Make sure to provide accurate details.

*A link to the online form will be activated during the official enrollment period. Please check our main website for announcements on specific dates.

2

Submission of Requirements

Once pre-registered, prepare and submit the necessary documents to the school's enrollment office. Ensure all copies are clear and complete. Our friendly staff will be on hand to assist you.

  • Original and photocopy of **Grade 10 Report Card (SF9)**
  • **Certificate of Good Moral Character**
  • **PSA Birth Certificate** (Original and photocopy)
  • **2x2 ID pictures** (2 pieces, white background)
  • Photocopy of **Parent's/Guardian's Valid ID**
  • (Optional) **ESC Certificate** or **QVR Certificate** for private school completers
3

Interview & Strand Confirmation

A brief interview will be conducted to discuss your academic interests, career goals, and to confirm your chosen Senior High School strand. This is also a great opportunity for you to ask any questions you may have about SVNHS.

4

Enrollment Confirmation

After successful submission of requirements and interview, your enrollment will be confirmed! You'll receive important details regarding orientation, class schedules, and school policies to get you ready for the academic year.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

When is the enrollment period? ▼

Official enrollment dates will be announced on our school's website and social media channels. Please stay tuned for updates!

Can I change my chosen strand after enrollment? ▼

Strand changes are subject to availability and school policy. It's best to finalize your choice during the interview process. For special cases, please consult with the SHS coordinator.

Are there any entrance exams? ▼

Currently, San Vicente National High School does not administer entrance exams for Senior High School enrollment. Admission is based on the submission of complete requirements and interview results.

Who can I contact for more questions? ▼

You can reach our enrollment office via email at info@yourschool.edu or call us at (123) 456-7890 during office hours. Our school is located in Panabo City, Davao Region.
